Phase 1

The goal for my site is honestly to infodump about undertale... half joking. The goal really is to show my love for Undertale however, and I want my design and organization to show that. As for audience, I'd say fellow indie game lovers, or anyone who needs convincing to play undertale :))


I don't believe I went above and beyond as I didn't really know HOW to... during phase 2 of the project, that's when I really want to experiment. Especially with fonts, colors, different formatting, etc. I won't change my grid style, but I may experiment with different ways to do it (such as adding rows... just some food for thought). I did use our old grid demo as a sort of starting point, but I want to be able to format it as I wish. I am using Phase 1 as a way to simply create a skeleton and a basis.
